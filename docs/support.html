Frequently asked questions

How do I change my player name?
Your name field is on the main menu, right above the Play button — just tap it and type a new one.

How do I change the board or card colors?
From the main menu, tap Settings, then choose a color under Background or Card Back.

How do I review the rules?
From the main menu, tap Settings, then "How to Play" to replay the tutorial anytime.

How do I start a new game?
Tap "New Game" from the main menu, or open the in-game menu and choose "Abandon Game."
